## 💡 Overview

<a href="#anyTrans">AnyTrans</a> is a comprehensive iOS device management tool for Mac that gives users complete, selective control over iPhone and iPad data — removing the all-or-nothing limitations that Apple's own transfer tools impose and enabling precise two-way transfer of any content category between iOS devices and Mac.

The <a href="#anyTrans">selective transfer engine</a> moves specific items in either direction without triggering a full sync — individual photos from a chosen album, a specific playlist, selected contacts, or particular message threads can transfer from iPhone to Mac or Mac to iPhone without affecting any other content on the device. Apple's built-in sync replaces device content with library content when categories overlap; AnyTrans transfers precisely what the user selects while leaving everything else untouched. The <a href="#anyTrans">complete content category coverage</a> handles photos, videos, music, podcasts, audiobooks, ringtones, messages, contacts, calendars, notes, app data, and every other data category that iPhone and iPad store — a unified management interface for all content types rather than separate tools for different data.

The <a href="#anyTrans">phone-to-phone migration</a> moves the complete content of one iPhone to another through the Mac — carrying contacts, messages, photos, app data, and settings from an old device to a new one with more selectivity and control than Apple's standard device setup migration. The <a href="#anyTrans">iCloud manager</a> browses and downloads content from iCloud backups without restoring to a device — extracting specific photos, messages, or contacts from an iCloud backup and saving them to Mac storage or transferring to a device for selective iCloud recovery.

<a href="#anyTrans">HEIC to JPEG automatic conversion</a> processes iPhone photos during transfer to Mac — converting Apple's HEIC format to universally compatible JPEG without a separate conversion step so transferred photos open in any application. <a href="#anyTrans">WhatsApp and messaging app data export</a> saves WhatsApp conversations, Line messages, and other messaging app content — exporting chat histories with attachments to readable PDF or HTML files that preserve years of conversation as accessible documents.

<a href="#anyTrans">Music library two-way management</a> adds music from Mac to iPhone without triggering a sync that replaces existing tracks, and copies music from iPhone to Mac for backup — the bidirectional music management that Apple restricts. <a href="#anyTrans">Custom ringtone creation and transfer</a> converts any audio file to iPhone ringtone format and transfers it directly — restoring a capability that Apple removed from iTunes years ago.
